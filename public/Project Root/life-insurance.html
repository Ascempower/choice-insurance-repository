Types of Life Insurance

Choice Insurance Agency offers comprehensive life insurance solutions to protect your loved ones' financial future.

Term Life Insurance

  • Affordable coverage for specific time periods
  • 10, 20, or 30-year terms available
  • Level premiums during term period
  • Convertible options available

Whole Life Insurance

  • Permanent coverage with cash value
  • Fixed premiums for life
  • Guaranteed death benefit
  • Tax-deferred cash value growth

Universal Life Insurance

  • Flexible premiums and death benefits
  • Cash value accumulation
  • Investment options available
  • Tax advantages

Why Choose Life Insurance?

  • Leave a legacy
