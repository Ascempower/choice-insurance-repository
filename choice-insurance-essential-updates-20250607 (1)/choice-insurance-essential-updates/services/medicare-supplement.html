Medicare Supplement Insurance (Medigap)

Medicare Supplement insurance, also known as Medigap, helps fill the "gaps" in Original Medicare coverage. These plans are designed to help pay some of the health care costs that Original Medicare doesn't cover, like copayments, coinsurance, and deductibles.

What Medicare Supplement Plans Cover

  • Medicare Part A coinsurance and hospital costs
  • Medicare Part B coinsurance or copayment
  • Blood (first 3 pints)
  • Part A hospice care coinsurance or copayment
  • Skilled nursing facility care coinsurance
  • Medicare Part A deductible
  • Medicare Part B deductible
  • Medicare Part B excess charges
  • Foreign travel emergency (up to plan limits)

Available Medigap Plans

There are 10 standardized Medigap plans available in most states, labeled A, B, C, D, F, G, K, L, M, and N. Each plan offers a different combination of benefits.
